The Third-Party Dashboard Assistant (TDA) is a unique feature that facilitates the involvement of family and friends in the matchmaking process without direct interaction with the profiles. Invited family and friends can view and select profiles that they find interesting and write comments about these profiles. These comments are private and can only be seen by the writer and the profile owner, ensuring that feedback is given discreetly and respectfully.

The TDA provides a separate interface for family and friends, allowing them to participate in the matchmaking process without disrupting the profile owner's experience. This tool helps maintain privacy and confidentiality while still enabling loved ones to share their opinions and preferences. By using the TDA, the profile owner can gather valuable insights and make more informed decisions about potential matches. This feature enhances the collaborative aspect of the platform, ensuring that the profile owner's journey is supported by trusted advisors in a structured and secure manner.

In summary, the Profile Notepad and the Third-Party Dashboard Assistant (TDA) are complementary tools that work together to provide a comprehensive and supportive matchmaking experience.

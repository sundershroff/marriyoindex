Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the role of a Profile Finder?

A Profile Finder at Marriyo Matrimony is responsible for identifying and compiling profiles that match the criteria set by clients. This role involves thorough research, verification of details, and presenting potential matches to clients.

2. How do I become a Profile Finder?

To become a Profile Finder, you need to sign up on our website/App and complete the registration process. This includes providing your personal information, agreeing to the terms and conditions, and, if required, paying the subscription fee.

3. What are the responsibilities of a Profile Finder?

A Profile Finder's responsibilities include:

  • Searching for potential matches based on client criteria
  • Verifying the authenticity and accuracy of profile details
  • Compiling and presenting profiles to clients
  • Maintaining confidentiality and privacy of all profiles
  • Following up with clients and providing additional assistance if needed

4. Is there a fee to become a Profile Finder?

Yes, there may be a subscription fee to access the full features and tools necessary for a Profile Finder. The fee structure is detailed on our website/App.

5. What information do I need to provide during registration?

During registration, you need to provide:

  • Full Name
  • Email ID (verified via OTP)
  • Mobile Number (verified via OTP)
  • Date of Birth
  • Government-issued ID (if applicable)

6. Can I access all profiles immediately after registering?

No, initially, profiles are blurred and interactions are limited. Full profile access requires a subscription. Once subscribed, you can access detailed profiles based on the plan you choose.

7. What types of subscription plans are available?

Subscription plans vary based on the level of access and additional features. Plans include:

  • Base
  • Middle
  • Upper Middle
  • Rich
  • Super Rich
  • Royal
Each plan offers different levels of access and features, which are detailed on our website/App.

8. How do I verify the profiles I find?

Verification involves cross-checking the provided information against official documents and records. You may need to contact the profile owner for additional details or request documents to verify their identity and credentials.

9. What if I find discrepancies in a profile?

If you find discrepancies, you should report them immediately. The profile will be re-evaluated, and necessary actions will be taken, including possible removal from the platform if the discrepancies are significant

10. How do I present profiles to clients?

Profiles are compiled and presented to clients through the platform's interface. You can create lists or individual profile presentations based on client preferences and criteria.

11. What support is available if I need help with my role?

Support is available through our Help section on the website/App. You can contact our support team via email or phone for any specific queries or assistance.

12. Can I refer friends to use the platform?

Yes, you can refer friends to use the platform. There are referral bonuses and incentives for successful referrals.

13. What are the benefits of being a Profile Finder?

Benefits include access to a wide network of profiles, tools to assist in finding and verifying potential matches, and opportunities to earn through successful profile matches and referrals.

14. How do I ensure the privacy and confidentiality of profiles?

Privacy and confidentiality are paramount. Ensure that all information is securely stored and shared only with authorized clients. Adhere to the platform’s privacy policies and guidelines strictly.

15. How do I update my Profile Finder account details?

You can update your account details through the account settings on the website/App. This includes updating personal information, contact details, and subscription plans

16. What is Marriyo?

Marriyo is a revolutionary matrimony platform designed by MARRIMMA Ai-TechLabs to transform the way individuals find their life partners. Utilizing advanced algorithms and a deep understanding of cultural nuances, Marriyo matches users based on compatibility, preferences, and values, ensuring meaningful connections in a secure and user-friendly environment.

17. How does Marriyo ensure privacy and security?

Marriyo prioritizes the privacy and security of its users by implementing robust measures such as prohibiting screenshots and screen recordings, ensuring profiles can only be viewed within the app, and incorporating built-in communication features that prevent the misuse of personal contact details. Users have full control over the information they choose to share, enhancing the safety of the matchmaking process.

18. What is the Profile Highlighter service?

The Profile Highlighter service enhances your profile's visibility by adding a distinctive visual emphasis, making it stand out in search results. This service is designed to attract more attention from potential matches, increasing your chances of receiving more interest and connecting with compatible partners.

19. How does the Profile Handle service work?

The Profile Handle service acts as a personal relationship manager, assisting you in managing your profile interactions and ensuring that all potential connections are promptly followed up. This service helps maintain momentum in your search for a partner by handling communication and coordination, allowing you to focus on building meaningful relationships.

20. What does the Private Investigator service offer?

The Private Investigator service conducts discreet background checks on potential matches to verify the authenticity and integrity of profiles. This service provides users with peace of mind by ensuring the accuracy of information provided, protecting them from deceptive profiles, and enhancing the overall safety of the matchmaking process.

21. How can the Booster Profile service benefit me?

The Booster Profile service significantly enhances your profile's visibility and reach by positioning it at the top of search results. The Happy Couples section showcases real-life success stories of couples who have found love through Marriyo. These testimonials inspire and motivate users by highlighting the effectiveness of the platform and the possibility of finding true love, fostering a supportive community.

The Refer a Friend program allows users to invite friends to join the Marriyo platform, expanding the community and creating more opportunities for connections. 26. What is the Profile Notepad?

The Profile Notepad is an innovative feature that allows profile owners to receive and review private comments from invited family and friends regarding specific profiles of interest. Each comment is read-only for the commenter and the profile owner, providing a confidential and organized way to consider various opinions and make informed decisions about potential matches.

27. How does the Third-Party Dashboard Assistant (TDA) work?

The Third-Party Dashboard Assistant (TDA) allows invited family and friends to view and select profiles of interest without direct interaction. They can write private comments that are only visible to the writer and the profile owner. This feature ensures family and friends can participate in the matchmaking process discreetly, providing valuable insights while maintaining privacy and confidentiality.

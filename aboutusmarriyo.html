About Us

Welcome to Marriyo

At Marriyo, we believe in the timeless value of traditional matchmaking, combined with the convenience and precision of modern technology. As a proud brand under MARRIMMA Ai-TechLabs India 1 (OPC) Private Limited, we are dedicated to helping individuals find their perfect life partners. Our slogan, "Best Choice for Best Life," reflects our commitment to creating meaningful and lasting connections.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the premier platform for traditional matrimony, where values and compatibility come together to create enduring relationships. We aim to help individuals embark on their journey towards a fulfilling and harmonious married life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and trusted matrimony service that respects cultural values and traditions while utilizing advanced technology to ensure precise matchmaking. We are dedicated to helping our members find their best match and begin their best life together.

What We Offer

  • Traditional Matchmaking:Marriyo brings the essence of traditional matchmaking into the digital age. Our platform respects and incorporates cultural and familial values, ensuring that our members find partners who share their beliefs and aspirations.

  • Advanced Technology: Leveraging state-of-the-art technology, Marriyo offers detailed profiles, advanced search filters, and compatibility algorithms to help members find their ideal match efficiently and accurately.

  • Personalized Services: We understand that each individual's journey to finding a life partner is unique. Marriyo provides personalized matchmaking services, offering expert guidance and support throughout the process to ensure a seamless experience.

  • Special Addons for Convenience:To enhance your experience and convenience, Marriyo offers special addons that cater to your needs and preferences. These addons are designed to provide additional support and make your journey towards finding the perfect partner even more enjoyable and stress-free.

Privacy and Security

At Marriyo, the security and privacy of our members are of utmost importance. To protect our users, we strictly prohibit screenshots and screen recordings while using our app. Profiles can only be viewed within the Marriyo app, ensuring the highest level of confidentiality. This feature is designed to safeguard our valuable customers, respecting their privacy as we would for our own family members. All communication features are built into the app to prevent misuse of personal and contact details. If members choose to share personal and contact information, they do so at their own risk, and Marriyo or our company will not be held responsible for any consequences.
